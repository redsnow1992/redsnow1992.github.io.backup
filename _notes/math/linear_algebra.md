*Matric form* \\

\left\{
  \begin{aligned}
 2x-y = 0 \\
-x+2y = 3 \\
  \end{aligned}
\right.
~~~~~~~~=>~~~~~~~~~~
\left[ 
  \begin{array}{cc} 
   2 & -1 \\
   -1 & 2 \\
  \end{array}
\right]

\left[
 \begin{array}{c}
  x \\
  y \\
  \end{array}
\right]  =
\left[
 \begin{array}{c}
  0 \\
  3 \\
  \end{array}
\right]  
$$
